Projects

While all my current projects are work-related, they showcase the skills and expertise I bring to my professional endeavors. Below are some key projects I have completed during my time contracted with DGSA:

App to access Material Safety Data Sheet

I developed an app using Microsoft Power Apps that launches when a QR code is scanned, displaying a material safety data sheet for all supplied goods.
By clicking on a product name, users can access a PDF stored on Microsoft SharePoint, providing more detailed information about the product.
This is a screenshot of what the app looked like!

Website to access Material Safety Data Sheet

Unfortunately, the app did not meet all the requirements, as it needed to be accessible to everyone in case of an emergency, and Power Apps only grants access to people within your organization.
With this limitation in mind, I decided to learn web development to recreate the app as a website.
If you want to check out the website, click here.

Website to access all Data Registers

With the success of the Safety Data Sheet Register website, additional registers were required to ensure immediate access for everyone. These new registers include:

  • MSDS
  • Plant & Equipment
  • Hired Plant & Equipment
  • Electrical
  • Fire Extinguisher
  • Lifting Gear

Work In Progress - Python script to convert Excel data into HTML

When I leave my current job, I want to ensure that this website remains up-to-date. To facilitate this for myself and anyone who succeeds me, I plan to develop a Python script that converts the relevant data from the chosen Excel register workbooks into HTML.
